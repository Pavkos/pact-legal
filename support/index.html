Support

Need help with Pact: Debt Payoff Planner? Contact support@pact.app.

Typical response time: 1-2 business days.

Before You Contact Us

  • Pact does not connect to banks. Add and update debts manually.
  • Planning dates are estimates based on the numbers and assumptions you enter.
  • Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store.
  • You can restore purchases from the Pro or Settings screen.
  • You can delete local app data from Settings.

What To Include

  • Your app version.
  • Your iOS version and device model.
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened.
  • A screenshot if it helps explain the issue.

Financial Disclaimer

Pact is a planning tool, not financial, investment, tax, legal, or credit advice. For personal financial decisions, consult a qualified professional.
